HIV Test

Mujer Organization offers free and confidential HIV testing to help you take control of your health. Our testing process is simple, quick, and private, with results available in as little as 20 minutes. Whether you choose a rapid test using a fingerstick or oral swab, or a standard blood test, our professional staff is here to support you throughout the entire process. After your test, we provide confidential counseling to discuss your results and offer guidance on next steps. Early detection is key, as it allows you to start treatment right away, which can keep you healthy and prevent the virus from progressing. Knowing your HIV status not only helps you take care of yourself but also allows you to protect your community by practicing safer behaviors. Get tested today—because your health matters to you, your loved ones, and your community.

COVID-19 Test

Mujer Organization provides free and confidential COVID-19 testing to help you stay safe and informed. Our testing services are fast, reliable, and designed to prioritize your privacy. Whether you need a rapid antigen test for quick results or a PCR test for added accuracy, our trained staff ensures a smooth and supportive experience. Knowing your COVID-19 status is a vital step in protecting yourself and others, especially during times of uncertainty. We also offer post-test guidance to help you make informed decisions about isolation, treatment, or preventative measures. Don’t wait—get tested today to safeguard your health and contribute to the safety of your community.
